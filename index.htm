About

I am a PhD Candidate at the L3S Research Center, Leibniz University Hannover, Germany, supervised by Prof. Wolfgang Nejdl.

My research focuses on Multilingual NLP, morphology-aware modeling, retrieval-augmented generation (RAG), and building equitable AI systems for underrepresented and morphologically rich languages.

I previously conducted doctoral research at Beijing University of Technology and served as Assistant Professor at Mekelle University, Ethiopia.
