About

I am a PhD Candidate at L3S Research Center, Leibniz University Hannover, Germany. My work focuses on multilingual NLP, low-resource languages, and retrieval-augmented generation. I also serve as a Research Associate at the University of Zurich , supporting computational linguistics and digital humanities research.

Research

My research focuses on Linguistically Grounded NLP Methods for Multilingual and Low-Resource Settings. I aim to improve large language models and retrieval-augmented systems by integrating linguistic knowledge, morphology-aware modeling, and multilingual representation techniques.

Currently, I am a Research Associate at the University of Zurich, contributing to interdisciplinary projects in computational linguistics and digital humanities. More info
