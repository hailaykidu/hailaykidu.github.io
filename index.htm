About Me

I am a PhD candidate at the L3S Research Center, Leibniz Universität Hannover, supervised by Prof. Wolfgang Nejdl and Prof. Niloy Ganguly, and a Research Associate at the University of Zurich (UZH). My research focuses on multilingual NLP, morphology‑aware tokenization, retrieval‑augmented generation (RAG), and responsible AI for underrepresented and structurally complex languages.

I have over a decade of experience in AI, computational linguistics, and machine learning, with a strong focus on building equitable and linguistically grounded NLP systems. I am the winner of the SIGIR 2025 LiveRAG Challenge and the AI Grid Hackathon 2024.

Research Interests

Research & Professional Experience

PhD Candidate — L3S Research Center, Leibniz Universität Hannover (2022–Present)

Research on multilingual NLP for low‑resource and morphologically rich languages. Developed linguistically informed tokenizers, embedding initialization strategies, and RAG pipelines (OpenSearch, Pinecone). Designed evaluation frameworks for compositionality, fairness, and infrastructure‑aware NLP. Contributed to EU Horizon proposals and interdisciplinary AI projects.

Research Associate — University of Zurich (UZH), Switzerland

Applied NLP to historical, scriptural, and digital humanities corpora. Integrated linguistic structure into language models and contributed to reproducible, inclusive NLP pipelines across disciplines.

PhD Student — Beijing University of Technology (2019–2021)

Conducted research in computational linguistics with a focus on morphologically rich and underrepresented languages, subword tokenization, and cross‑lingual adaptation.

Assistant Professor — Mekelle University, University of Gondar & Adigrat University (2009–2019)

Taught Information Retrieval, NLP, Algorithms, and Information Systems. Supervised MSc theses on Amharic text mining and morphological analysis. Established early NLP research groups in Ethiopia and modernized AI curricula.

Data Scientist / ML Engineer — Cyber Software, Ethiopia (2007–2009)

Developed national‑scale decision‑support systems for Ethiopia’s justice and public administration sectors.
